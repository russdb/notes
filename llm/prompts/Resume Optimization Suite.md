Step 0 | Upload Resume (Required First Step)
───────────────────────────────────────────
Please use the attached resume

After the resume is uploaded, I will ask you to perform one or more of the following numbered operations:


----
1 | Rewrite My Resume Like a Recruiter
Focus on measurable outcomes, senior-level impact language, and engaging yet professional phrasing.
Preserve every fact exactly as it appears in the uploaded resume.

2 | Make It ATS-Proof
Strategically incorporate relevant keywords and standard industry terminology — without keyword stuffing or altering facts.

3 | Tear It Apart (So It’s Better)
Deliver direct, candid, constructive feedback on the current resume.
Clearly identify weaknesses, what is merely adequate, and what significantly reduces appeal to recruiters/hiring managers.
Provide specific, actionable improvement recommendations based solely on the content present.

4 | Craft a Cover Letter That Pops
Build a coherent career narrative using only facts from the uploaded resume.
Highlight motivation, key impact, and differentiation — without fabricating achievements or experiences.

5 | Tailor It to the Job
Tailor the resume point-by-point to match this specific job posting:
Align phrasing, emphasis, order, and keyword usage to the role’s stated requirements while remaining 100% factually accurate to the uploaded resume.

6 | Fix My Resume Gaps
Emphasize transferable skills, professional development, leadership, or measurable outcomes that DO appear in the resume.
Do not create new activities, training, or accomplishments to fill the gap.

7 | Build a LinkedIn Bio That Gets Noticed
Write a strong LinkedIn About section that positions me as credible, professional, and ready for my next opportunity.
Use only information explicitly present in the uploaded resume.

Which step(s) would you like me to execute first? (You can request multiple.)
